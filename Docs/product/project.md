## High Level description of the project for context and help creating the PRD

OpenClub is an AI native club management Software for racket sports where members can book and manage reservations/lessons via text message through a conversational ai agent that communicates with our backend


## Users

There are two main users for this application. Both have different use cases 

### Member: 

A member is a person who uses the club facilities. A customer

1. Communicates with our agent via text message (ideally whatsapp)
2. Can ask for their current reservations and all members in the reservation
3. Can create a reservation
4. Can book a lesson with a pro
5. Can ask about available programs and their dates. Ability to ask for a specific program such as an event, an open play, or a 


### Club Admin:

A club admin is in charge of managing the club. They should have access to information such as total reservations, court usage, trends, etc. Full insights and information they have access to will be decided later on

A club admin can:

1. Create any type of program (event, open play, clinic). Set the price of this program, the date and time, and assign pro's to it
2. Look up a member to look at their reservations, membership level, retention, etc
3. More coming later


### Pro:

A pro works in the club and is in charge of giving lessons or clinics. A pro is a coach. 

1. Can look at all lessons that members have scheduled with them
2. Can accept/decline a lesson request
3. Can create a lesson themselves and add a person to it



## Programs

An program can be one of the following

### Open Play

An open play is an open court where players can individually sign in . There is usually a pro assigned to the open play who monitors the coach. But this is not a lesson/clinic, just match play. Cost is usually lower side


### Clinic

A clinic is basically a group lesson. The court is blocked and players can individually sign up. The clinic is assigned to the pro who gives it

### Event

An event can be any custom event created by a club admin. Could be a celebration, "mens night", 4th of july open play, etc. Pricing and times vary between events
